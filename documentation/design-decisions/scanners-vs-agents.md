# Scanners vs Agents

why not just let agents do everything?

Well...

Scanners are fast ways to take well known issues and highlight them. This is where you will find a bunch of quick wins.

We take this, that has been industry standard and we use the agent system to augment it.

Agents end up looking into areas we couldn't predict, or areas that the scanners missed. Though a lot of times they just confirm what the scanners found already haha.
